My Anime List

Here are some of my favorite anime series and movies. Whether you're new to anime or a seasoned fan, check out these recommendations!

  • Death Note – A psychological thriller about justice and power.
  • Attack on Titan – Humanity's fight for survival against titans.
  • Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood – A journey of two brothers seeking redemption.
  • Steins;Gate – A mind-bending story about time travel.
  • One Piece – Epic adventures on the high seas with Luffy and his crew.
  • Jujutsu Kaisen – Modern sorcery and intense battles.
  • Mob Psycho 100 – Supernatural comedy with heart.
  • Your Name – A beautiful, emotional movie about fate and connection.
  • Demon Slayer – Stunning animation and powerful storytelling.
  • Spy x Family – A fun blend of action, comedy, and family.
